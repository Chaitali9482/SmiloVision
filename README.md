SmiloVision is a smart AI-powered dental health app designed to analyze smiles and detect common oral issues using advanced image processing and machine learning. It helps users maintain better dental hygiene by offering instant insights, guidance, and appointment support.

AI Smile Scan: Users can upload a photo of their teeth or smile. The app scans it to identify possible dental conditions like plaque, stains, gum issues, or cavities.
Report Generation: Provides a simple, visual report showing dental health status and suggestions.
Appointment Booking: Connects users with nearby dentists for online or in-person consultations.
Daily Care Tips: Personalized tips and reminders for brushing, flossing, and mouthwash routines.
Progress Tracking: Tracks smile improvements over time through images and data.
